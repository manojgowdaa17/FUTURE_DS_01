# FUTURE_DS_01 - Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project is an interactive **Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard** developed as part of the Future Intern Data Science Internship program.
The dashboard provides business insights through multiple visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs), enabling data-driven decision making and performance tracking.

##  Features
- Executive business overview
- Revenue trend analysis
- Product performance analysis
- Regional sales analysis
- Category-wise performance tracking
- Product comparison section
- Business insights and recommendations
- Interactive charts and visualizations
- Responsive dashboard design
- Sidebar navigation for easy access

##  Dashboard Components
### 1. Executive Overview
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Units Sold
- Average Order Value
- Best Performing Product
- Best Performing Region

### 2. Revenue Analysis
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Monthly Order Trend

### 3. Product Performance
- Revenue by Product
- Units Sold by Product

### 4. Regional Analysis
- Revenue by Region
- Order Distribution by Region

### 5. Category Analysis
- Category-wise Revenue Contribution

### 6. Comparison Center
- Product Comparison
- Revenue vs Units Sold Comparison

### 7. Trend Analysis
- Monthly Growth Percentage
- Revenue Growth Trends

### 8. Business Insights
- Performance observations
- Strategic findings

### 9. Recommendations
- Inventory recommendations
- Marketing recommendations
- Business expansion suggestions

The project uses a synthetic business sales dataset containing:
- 1000 sales records
- Multiple product categories
- Regional sales data
- Revenue and quantity metrics
- Monthly business performance information
